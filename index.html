A community for folks working in SaaS post sales

Account Managers, Customer Success, Customer Support, Implementation, Onboarding, Technical Account Managers, and everyone inbetween. We want to create a community where you feel welcome to network, to build your network, not to be sold to or recruited.

Who you are

We want a super diverse range of people in post sales roles. If you're in:

  • Account Management
  • Customer Enablement
  • Customer Success
  • Customer Support
  • Help Documentation
  • Implementation
  • Onboarding
  • Technical Account Management

then we'd love for you to join the community and come along to one of our events! We can't wait to meet you.

Why?

During lockdown, a lot of meet-ups and communities moved to online, and once we started to venture out, these didn't come back. During the series of layoffs that happened in late 2022 to now, we've been searching for a community of likeminded post-sales people, and could only find brand sponsored events.

Now, there's nothing wrong with a brand sponsored event - we're just looking to create a community where the goal is to build a support network of peers without the pressure to buy the latest CS software.
